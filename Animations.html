Prikkel voor de pret

Prikkel voor de pret is a game in progress made during my gradiuation fase at HKU, The game is a Local multiplayer Party game where players can play different types of minigames using there phone as a controller. On a shared screen (Tv,laptop or tablet) players can competie against each other. In Prikkel voor de pret you can experience playing togheter in a whole new way, it uses the sensors of your phone like a bit like a wii remote

if you would like to try the game, their wil be a alpha-build soon on the google play store! i am still working on releasing builds for multiple platforms and improving the gameplay and systems.
