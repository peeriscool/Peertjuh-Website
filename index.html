About me:

Welcome to my website! My name is peer i'm a game developer and artist from the netherlands. when i was growing up i discovered Blender3D and i fell in love with making 3D models which led me to go study Game Art at Sint Lucas. from there i discovered a whole world of creative technical people, software and hardware after that i never wanted anything else than to create experiences using technology.

    I have Studied:
  • Media Design / Game Art at Sint Lucas Eindhoven
  • Game Development at HKU Utrecht
